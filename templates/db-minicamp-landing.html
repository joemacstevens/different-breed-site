Sports Camp in Teaneck, NJ

We're not your average camp.

Our youth sports camp in Teaneck serves kids from Bergen County, Englewood, Fort Lee, and surrounding areas. Campers work with certified coaches in boxing, strength training, and sports performance to build skill, confidence, and coordination. Each day is structured to keep them engaged, active, and challenged.

🥊

Boxing Training

Real fundamentals from coaches who've competed. Stance, footwork, combinations, defense. We teach discipline through the sweet science.

💪

Strength & Conditioning

Age-appropriate training that builds power and endurance. Bodyweight exercises, core work, agility drills. No fluff.

🎯

Character Development

The Three Ds: Dedication, Desire, Discipline. Life lessons that carry beyond the gym. Mental toughness, goal setting, resilience.

🏃

Sports Performance

Speed, agility, coordination drills used in baseball, soccer, basketball. Athletic development that translates to any sport.

🍎

Nutrition Education

Teaching young athletes how to fuel properly. Healthy snacks provided. Hydration emphasized. Knowledge they'll use for life.

👥

Small Groups

Limited spots for maximum attention. We know every kid's name. Personalized coaching. That's the Different Breed way.
