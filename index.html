What We Do

We're not your average camp.

Campers work with certified coaches in boxing, strength training, and sports performance to build skill, confidence, and coordination. Each day is structured to keep them engaged, active, and challenged.

Boxing Training

Real fundamentals from coaches who've competed. Stance, footwork, combinations, defense. We teach discipline through the sweet science.

Strength & Conditioning

Age-appropriate training that builds power and endurance. Bodyweight exercises, core work, agility drills. No fluff.

Character Development

The Three Ds: Dedication, Desire, Discipline. Life lessons that carry beyond the gym. Mental toughness, goal setting, resilience.

Sports Performance

Speed, agility, coordination drills used in baseball, soccer, basketball. Athletic development that translates to any sport.

Flexibility Training

Dynamic mobility sessions in the Pulse Studio keep athletes loose, improve range of motion, and prep their bodies for the next workout.

Friends Who Grind

Small squads mean every drill stays high-energy and fun. Kids build friendships fast while competing, laughing, and pushing each other forward.
